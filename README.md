# 🎬 TikTok Tycoon: Live Shopping Empire

> Can you turn $1M into a live-streaming empire in 36 months? Choose products, hire influencers, manage budgets — and watch your fortune rise or fall in real-time.

## 🎮 Play Now

**[👉 Play the Game](https://your-username.github.io/tiktok-tycoon/game.html)** *(replace with your actual link)*

## ✨ What Makes It Fun

- **Real Business Decisions**: Pick from beauty, fashion, electronics, or food. Each choice matters.
- **Influencer Drama**: Hire from micro-influencers to mega-stars. Can you afford the top tier?
- **Live Emotions**: Watch your profits explode 🎉 or crash 😰 with animated results
- **Achievement System**: Unlock milestones like "First Profit" and "Comeback King"
- **Save & Archive**: Track every 36-month journey. Compare your best runs.

## 🚀 Features

- 📊 **36-Month Campaign** — Survive 3 years of market chaos
- 💰 **Dynamic Economics** — Traffic, conversion, margins all shift monthly
- 🎯 **Strategic Depth** — Balance ad spend, product margins, and influencer costs
- 🏆 **Achievements** — Earn badges for hitting profit milestones
- 💾 **Auto-Save** — Your empire is saved automatically
- 📜 **Career Archive** — Review all your past 36-month runs

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Choose a Product** — Beauty? Fashion? Electronics? Food?
2. **Pick Your Influencer** — From $50K micro-stars to $500K mega-celebs
3. **Set Your Budget** — Ad spend, product margins, marketing tactics
4. **Hit "Go Live"** — Watch the numbers roll in (or crash)
5. **Survive 36 Months** — Can you reach the $10M profit goal?

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- Pure HTML + Vanilla JavaScript
- TailwindCSS for styling
- LocalStorage for saves
- Zero dependencies, runs anywhere

## 📦 Run Locally

```bash
# Just open the file
open game.html

# Or serve it
python3 -m http.server 8000
# Visit http://localhost:8000/game.html
```
